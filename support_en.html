Welcome to the SpeakIt support center. Here you'll find answers to frequently asked questions and guidance on using our app.

Getting Started

SpeakIt is a voice note application that helps you record ideas and information through speech, with AI-assisted transcription capabilities. Here's how to get started:

  1. Launch the SpeakIt app on your device
  2. Grant microphone permissions when prompted
  3. Tap the record button to start capturing your voice
  4. Tap again to stop recording
  5. Wait briefly for the AI to transcribe your speech
  6. Review and edit the transcribed text as needed

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why does SpeakIt need microphone access?

A: SpeakIt requires microphone access to record your voice for transcription. Without this permission, the app cannot capture audio for conversion to text. We only access your microphone when you explicitly start a recording session.

Q: How accurate is the voice-to-text transcription?

A: The accuracy of transcription depends on several factors including speech clarity, background noise, accent, and technical terms used. For best results, speak clearly in a quiet environment. You can always edit the transcribed text afterward.

Q: Can I edit my recordings or transcriptions?

A: Yes, SpeakIt allows you to edit your transcribed text after conversion. Simply tap on the transcribed text and make your changes.

Q: Where are my recordings stored?

A: Your recordings and transcriptions are stored locally on your device. We prioritize your privacy and security.

Q: How can I delete my recordings?

A: To delete a recording, navigate to your list of notes, swipe left on the note you wish to delete, and tap the delete button. You can also open a note and use the delete option in the menu.

Q: Is there a time limit for recordings?

A: SpeakIt supports recordings of various lengths, but for optimal transcription results, we recommend keeping individual recordings under 10 minutes.

Troubleshooting

Microphone not working

If your microphone isn't working with SpeakIt:

  • Ensure you've granted microphone permissions in your device settings
  • Check that no other apps are currently using your microphone
  • Restart your device and try again
  • Make sure your device's microphone isn't physically blocked or covered

Transcription errors

If you're experiencing frequent transcription errors:

  • Try recording in a quieter environment
  • Speak clearly and at a moderate pace
  • Hold your device closer to your mouth when speaking
  • For technical terms or unusual names, you may need to edit the transcription afterward

App crashes or freezes

If SpeakIt crashes or freezes:

  • Close the app completely and restart it
  • Ensure your device has sufficient storage space
  • Update to the latest version of SpeakIt from the App Store
  • If problems persist, try reinstalling the app
